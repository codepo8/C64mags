Wanted!

If you have any of the following disk and paper magazines, please contact Jazzcat for submission to this site!

Requested format:

Disk magazines: converted to PC and sent in email or via snail mail (address obtainable via email). All C64 formats accepted (e.g. .prg, .d64, .d81, .lnx, 4-zip, .nib etc.)

Paper magazines, disk covers and votesheets - keep in mind we are looking for better quality versions of what we have in the archive too! - scan in black & white at 300 DPI or above. Colour scans for colour pages are welcome also.
